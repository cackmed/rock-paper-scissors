Rock Paper Scissors!

Rules:

  • To start select one of the options below and click the play button.
  • You can select one of three options, rock, paper, or scissors.
  • In order to win, what you choose needs to beat the option chosen by the computer.
  • Rock beats Scissors via crushing, Scissors beat Paper via cutting, and Paper beats Rock via covering.
  • If you and the computer both choose the same option, it will result in a draw.
  • Good Luck!
